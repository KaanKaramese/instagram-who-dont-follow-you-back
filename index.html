Instagram Unfollowers Checker

How to see who doesn't follow you back

  1. Go to Instagram on your computer and open Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  2. Request your data export. When you receive the ZIP file, extract it.
  3. Find followers_1.json and following.json in connections/followers_and_following/.
  4. Upload both files below to see who you follow that doesn't follow you back.
